Who We Are.

The Driving Force

AIESEC in USJ

Based in the University of Sri Jayewardenepura, we are a global youth-led organization striving to achieve peace and fulfillment of humankind's potential. We are dedicated to creating impactful initiatives that empower Sri Lankan youth, build leadership qualities, and drive tangible social change through cross-cultural exchanges and local projects.

Our Public Relation Impact

Official Healthcare Partner

George Steuarts Health

A pioneer in the Sri Lankan pharmaceutical industry, dedicated to enhancing the quality of life through accessible, world-class healthcare solutions. Instead of a financial handover, upon the completion of our 2,000KM collective goal, George Steuarts Health has pledged to donate essential medicines and healthcare resources directly to a local health foundation/hospital, ensuring tangible relief reaches those who need it most.

Got Questions?

THE FINE PRINT.

Can I use a smartwatch?

Yes. As long as it syncs your data directly to Apple Health or Google Fit, you can take a screenshot of the main app and upload it.

What if I forget a day?

You lose your streak and are disqualified from winning the individual top prizes. However, your steps still count towards the 2,000KM charity donation!

Who can join?

Literally everyone. We have separate leaderboards for the general public, university students, and internal AIESEC members.

How are steps verified?

Our OC team manually verifies the screenshots uploaded. Any signs of tampering, renaming, or manual entry logs will result in an immediate ban from the leaderboard.

When is the donation made?

Once we collectively cross the 2,000KM mark, the official handover of medicine and healthcare resources will take place at the conclusion of World Health Week.
